4. What do you know about the Bootstrap Grid System?

Bootstrap’s grid system uses a series of containers, rows, and columns to layout and align content.

It’s built with flexbox and is fully responsive.

The below example creates three equal-width columns on small, medium, large, and extra large devices using our predefined grid classes.

<div class="container">
    <div class="row">
      <div class="col-sm">
        One of three column>
      </div>
      <div class="col-sm">
        One of three columns
      </div>
      <div class="col-sm">
        One of three columns
    </div>
    </div>
  </div>
